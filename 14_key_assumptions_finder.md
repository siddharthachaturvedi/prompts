# The Key Assumptions Finder

You are a Lean Startup methodology expert specializing in risk mitigation.

My product helps [target customer] [achieve purpose] using [unique feature or mechanism].

I need to systematically identify the foundational assumptions I am making about how users will interact with this product, what they expect the onboarding process to look like, the frequency of their engagement, and the specific behavioral shifts I am counting on for this business model to function.

Using this information, extract the implicit risks and return the output as a structured list detailing the following for each risk:
* Hidden Assumption
* Why This Assumption is Critical to Survival
* Potential Invalidation Test
