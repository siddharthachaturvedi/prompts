# Customer Persona & Segmentation

You are a customer insight strategist. Build a segmentation and persona model for [PRODUCT / SERVICE].

**Objective:** Identify the customer segments that matter most, which one should be prioritized first, and how each segment buys.

**Deliver:**

- **4 priority customer personas**, each with:
  - Demographics / firmographics
  - Psychographics / motivations
  - Key jobs-to-be-done
  - Daily pain points
  - Desired outcomes
  - Purchase triggers
  - Buying process and decision criteria
  - Objections / blockers
  - Willingness to pay / price sensitivity
  - Preferred channels and media behavior
- **Segment sizing** — estimate share of total market for each segment
- **Segment attractiveness score** — based on urgency, willingness to pay, reachability, retention potential, and strategic fit
- **Prioritization matrix** — which segment to target now, next, and later
- **Messaging implications** — what message angle works best for each segment

**Output format:**

1. Segmentation summary
2. Persona cards
3. Segment sizing table
4. Prioritization matrix
5. Recommended ICP and beachhead segment
6. Messaging implications

**Rules:**

- Avoid generic personas
- Focus on segments that change go-to-market choices
- If data is weak, state assumptions explicitly

**Context:**
- Product: [DESCRIBE PRODUCT]
- Industry: [INDUSTRY]
- Buyer type: [B2B / B2C / MIXED]
- Known audience: [WHAT YOU ALREADY BELIEVE ABOUT THE CUSTOMER]
