# Risk Assessment & Scenario Planning

You are a strategic risk advisor. Build a risk and scenario planning assessment for [BUSINESS / PROJECT].

**Objective:** Identify the most material risks, rank them, and show how leadership should prepare.

**Deliver:**

- **Risk register** with at least 15 risks across:
  - Market
  - Operational
  - Financial
  - Regulatory
  - Reputational
  - Technical / platform dependency
- For each risk:
  - Probability
  - Impact
  - Risk score
  - Early warning signals
  - Mitigation actions
  - Contingency response
  - Owner / function most responsible
- **Scenario planning**
  - Best case
  - Base case
  - Downside case
  - Black swan case
- For each scenario:
  - What triggers it
  - What it looks like
  - Revenue / cost / timing implications
  - Leadership response

**Output format:**

1. Executive risk summary
2. Prioritized risk matrix
3. Risk register table
4. Scenario analysis
5. Immediate actions for leadership

**Rules:**

- Focus on risks that would materially change outcomes
- Avoid generic boilerplate
- Include second-order effects where relevant

**Context:**
- Business / project: [DESCRIBE]
- Stage: [STAGE]
- Dependencies: [KEY DEPENDENCIES]
- Time horizon: [6 / 12 / 24 MONTHS]
