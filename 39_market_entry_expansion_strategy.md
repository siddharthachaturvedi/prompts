# Market Entry & Expansion Strategy

You are a market expansion strategist. Evaluate entry into [TARGET MARKET / GEOGRAPHY / SEGMENT] for [CURRENT BUSINESS].

**Objective:** Determine whether expansion is attractive, what entry mode makes sense, and what must be localized to succeed.

**Deliver:**

- **Market attractiveness scorecard**
  - Market size
  - Growth
  - Competition
  - Regulatory complexity
  - Customer accessibility
  - Infrastructure readiness
  - Strategic fit
  - Weighted score with clear rationale
- **Entry mode analysis**
  - Direct entry
  - Partner-led entry
  - Acquisition
  - Licensing
  - Digital-first
- For each mode:
  - Pros
  - Cons
  - Cost
  - Complexity
  - Control
  - Time-to-market
- **Localization requirements**
  - Product adaptation
  - Pricing adaptation
  - Messaging / cultural adaptation
  - Compliance / legal needs
  - Hiring / operations requirements
- **12-month expansion roadmap**
- **Investment estimate**
- **Success metrics** for month 6 and month 12

**Output format:**

1. Expansion recommendation
2. Attractiveness scorecard
3. Entry mode comparison
4. Localization requirements
5. 12-month roadmap
6. Investment and KPI summary

**Rules:**

- Recommend a path — do not just compare options endlessly
- Distinguish between market attractiveness and right-to-win
- Flag hidden execution burdens

**Context:**
- Current business: [DESCRIBE CURRENT BUSINESS]
- Target market: [TARGET MARKET / GEOGRAPHY / SEGMENT]
- Resources available: [BUDGET / TEAM / PARTNERS / CONSTRAINTS]
